Catalog Overview

Trims & Accessories, Specified and Delivered

Five product and service categories cover the components most garment orders require, from the first stitch to the final hang tag.

Category 01

Sewing Threads & Trims

Polyester core-spun and cotton sewing threads in mill-verified, buyer-matched shades, alongside narrow trims such as elastics, piping, and binding tape.

Available in cone and spool formats sized for both industrial sewing lines and sample rooms.

Request a Sample
Category 02

Zippers & Fasteners

Nylon coil, metal-tooth, and invisible zippers in standard and custom lengths, plus snap fasteners and sliders for outerwear, denim, and bags.

Each batch is pull-tested and checked for slider function before it leaves our facility.

Request a Sample
Category 03

Buttons & Hardware

Shirt buttons, coat buttons, rivets, and D-rings in resin, metal, and horn-effect finishes, sorted and packed to buyer count and carton specification.

Custom logo-embossed buttons are available with a molded sample turnaround of one week.

Request a Sample
Category 04

Labels & Packaging

Woven and printed main labels, satin care labels, and swing/hang tags, matched to buyer artwork and washing-instruction standards.

Packaging accessories include polybags, cartons, and barcode-ready sticker sets for export shipments.

Request a Sample
Category 05

Full-Package Sourcing

For buyers who prefer a single point of contact, ATABD consolidates threads, fasteners, labels, and packaging into one coordinated order, inspected and freight-ready as a complete set.

Consolidated Ordering

One purchase order covers every trim category needed for a style, reducing vendor management overhead.

Pre-Shipment Inspection

A single QC pass checks all accessory categories together against your tech pack before goods leave Dhaka.

Freight Coordination

We arrange consolidated freight to your factory or forwarder, timed against your production schedule.
