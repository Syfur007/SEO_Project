About Atlantic Attire

Fifteen Years of Getting Trims Right

A Dhaka-based supplier built around one job: making sure the small components of a garment never hold up the production line.

Our Story

From a Trims Counter to a Total Garments Solution

Atlantic Attire (ATABD) started as a small trims counter serving local tailoring workshops in Dhaka. As Bangladesh's export garment sector grew, so did our catalog — from a handful of thread colors to a full range of sewing threads, zippers, buttons, labels, and packaging accessories used by manufacturers and buying houses nationwide.

Today, ATABD operates as a total garments solution provider: one supplier coordinating the sourcing, inspection, and delivery of the components that hold a finished garment together.

How We Work

Our Order Process

Every order — sample or bulk — follows the same five-step process, so buying houses know exactly what to expect at each stage.

  1. Inquiry & Tech Pack Review

    Share your trim card or tech pack; our team confirms specification, MOQ, and indicative pricing.

  2. Sampling

    Approved samples are dispatched within 72 hours for buyer sign-off before bulk production begins.

  3. Bulk Production

    Confirmed orders are scheduled against our stockholding and supplier network to meet your ship date.

  4. Quality Control

    Every batch is inspected against the approved sample and buyer specification sheet prior to packing.

  5. Shipment & Handover

    Goods are packed, labeled, and coordinated for delivery to your factory floor or freight forwarder.
